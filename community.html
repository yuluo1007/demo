Roles & Program

Community Roles & Program

At AgentScope, we believe that a thriving ecosystem is built by people. Whether you are a developer, a writer, or an organizer, there is a place for you to shine. Explore our community roles below and find the path that matches your passion.

Heroes

AgentScope Heroes

Heroes are the core development force behind our projects. You drive high-quality iterations through code contributions, module maintenance, and feature development.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Core DevelopmentContribute high-quality code to core modules.
  • MaintenanceAssist in maintaining project stability and performance.
  • Feature ImplementationLead or participate in the development of major features.

How to Join

  • NominationNominated by Core Developers / Maintainers within the specific project.
  • PR QualityReasonable design, coding standards, maintainability, test/documentation coverage, and responsiveness to reviews.
  • PR QuantityConsistent contribution frequency, coverage of key modules, and contribution ratio to versions/milestones.

Incentives

  • Direct AccessDirect communication channels with the Core Team and Maintainers.
  • Roadmap InfluenceSubstantial influence on the technical roadmap.
  • Priority ReviewPriority review channel for your Pull Requests.
  • CertificationExclusive Hero Honor Certificate (digital & physical).
Super-Heroes

QwenPaw Super-Heroes

Super Heroes are the deepest participants and guardians of the QwenPaw project. Beyond contributing, you take responsibility for architecture control and code quality. As a core governance force, you hold PR Review permissions to guard the quality of every line of code entering the main branch.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Code GovernanceExercise PR Review (CR) permissions on the QwenPaw GitHub repository.
  • Quality ControlParticipate in key version releases and quality gatekeeping.
  • ArchitectureProvide professional oversight on architecture and code quality.

How to Join

  • PrerequisiteMust already hold the AgentScope · QwenPaw Hero identity.
  • NominationNominated by the QwenPaw Core Development Team.
  • TermThis role has a fixed term and is subject to periodic review.

Incentives

  • PermissionsGitHub PR Review (CR) permissions for the QwenPaw repository.
  • GovernanceParticipation rights in key version releases and quality gates.
  • StrategyHigher priority participation in the technical roadmap.
  • CertificationExclusive Super Hero Honor Certificate (digital & physical).
Guides

AgentScope Guides

Guides are the lighthouses of community learning. You help newcomers grow faster and promote knowledge retention through Q&A, tutorials, and best-practice sharing.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • MentorshipAnswer questions in community channels and help members troubleshoot.
  • Knowledge SharingWrite tutorials, articles, case studies, or host learning sessions.
  • Asset CreationContribute reusable assets like FAQs, troubleshooting guides, templates, and learning paths.

How to Join

  • NominationNominated by Core Developers / Maintainers within the specific project.
  • ActivityHigh engagement in discussion groups, consistent online Q&A, and quality of assistance.
  • Content OutputQuality and quantity of tutorials, articles, cases, and streams (clear structure, reproducible, shareable).
  • Best PracticesContributions to FAQs, debugging guides, template projects, etc.

Incentives

  • IdentityOfficial "Guides" Certification (digital & physical).
  • Early AccessQualification for beta-testing new features.
  • ExposureHigh-quality content recommended via official AgentScope channels.
Ambassadors

AgentScope Ambassadors

Ambassadors are the voice of AgentScope. Active both online and offline, you bring AgentScope to a wider audience through content creation, community events, and local promotion.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Content CreationProduce high-quality blogs, videos, or tutorials related to AgentScope.
  • Public SpeakingShare knowledge at meetups, conferences, or webinars.
  • Community BuildingOrganize or co-organize local or online AgentScope events.

How to Join

  • ApplicationSubmit your application through the application link.
  • Content CreatorPublished at least 1 blog/video/tutorial related to AgentScope or agent-based topics.
  • SpeakerDelivered at least 1 talk related to AgentScope or agent-based topics at a meetup, conference, or webinar.
  • OrganizerOrganized or co-organized at least 1 event related to AgentScope or agent-based topics.
  • ReviewApplications are reviewed by the AgentScope Team.

Incentives

  • Official SupportAccess to official event resources and organizational support.
  • Early InsightsFirst-hand knowledge of product strategy and new directions.
  • Marketing BoostPromotion of your activities via official AgentScope channels.
  • CertificationOfficial Ambassador Certificate (digital & physical).
